📖 How to Use

Section 1 – Date of SL:
Pick your Study Leave date then tap Calculate. Choose Current Date to compare with today, or Specify Date to compare with any date.

Section 2 – Date of Claim:
Same as Section 1 but for your Claim date.

Section 3 – SL to Claim:
Compares SL date vs Claim date directly. Auto-fills from Sections 1 & 2.

Status Indicators:

  • 🟢 Past – Date already occurred
  • 🔵 Same Day – Matches the compared date
  • 🔴 Advanced Claim – Claim filed before SL happened

Export CSV: Downloads all results as a .csv file openable in Excel.

Get Excel Template: Downloads a pre-built .xls file with live formulas. Type dates in yellow cells.
